About Ascend

Episerver Ascend is an annual user conference that will host around 800 digital marketers and commerce leaders.

Ascend will feature nearly 100 sessions across six separate and comprehensive persona-based tracks, including hands-on labs, customer led presentations, and product and industry expert discussions.

There will also be a solutions and partner exhibition hall, an Epi Code Bash competition, and a panel of industry analyst guest speakers and Episerver Digital Ninety-Nines.
